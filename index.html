About Me

Hi there! My name is Sheila and I am currently a senior at Florida International University (FIU). I am pursuing a dual-degree with a B.S in computer science and a B.S in applied mathematics. I am currently serving as a research assistant working with data cleaning in mobile wireless sensor networks under the mentorship of Dr. Niki Pissinou.

My expected graduation date is Spring 2019 where I plan on applying directly for my PhD so that I can start working on my own exciting research. My main research interest is in artificial intelligence but I enjoy anything that allows me to think creatively and analytically. I'm an avid learner and enjoy partaking in opportunities that allow me to learn new things. A PDF of my resume here.
